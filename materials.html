Materials

Natural & Sustainable Materials

At SUKBIT, we believe that beautiful fashion should start with beautiful, sustainable materials. Every bag we create is handwoven from carefully selected natural fibers that are not only eco-friendly but also incredibly durable and beautiful.

Abaca Fiber

Known as "Manila hemp," abaca is one of the strongest natural fibers in the world. Sourced from the leaf sheaths of the abaca plant, this material gives our bags exceptional durability while remaining lightweight and flexible.

Palm Leaves

Sustainably harvested palm leaves provide excellent flexibility and natural water resistance. The fibers are carefully processed to maintain their strength while creating beautiful textures and patterns.

Rattan Strips

Rattan adds structural integrity to our bags while maintaining flexibility. This renewable material grows rapidly in tropical forests and can be harvested without harming the plant.

Buri Palm

Buri palm fibers are known for their fine texture and natural luster. These fibers create smooth, elegant surfaces that are both beautiful to touch and visually striking.

Material Properties

Lightweight

Despite their strength, our materials are incredibly light, making our bags comfortable to carry all day.

Durable

Natural fibers provide exceptional strength and longevity, ensuring your bag lasts for years.

Breathable

Natural materials allow air circulation, preventing moisture buildup and odors.

Biodegradable

All our materials are 100% natural and will return to the earth without harmful environmental impact.

Water-Resistant

Natural oils in the fibers provide inherent water resistance, protecting your belongings.

Unique Texture

Each fiber has its own distinctive texture, making every bag truly one-of-a-kind.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We source all our materials from sustainable farms and local communities that practice responsible harvesting. No trees are cut down, and all plants continue to grow and produce fibers year after year.

Our dyeing process uses natural, plant-based dyes whenever possible, minimizing environmental impact while creating the beautiful, rich colors that SUKBIT is known for.

By choosing natural materials over synthetic alternatives, we reduce our carbon footprint and support traditional agricultural communities that have been harvesting these fibers for generations.

Material Care

Each material has its own unique characteristics, and proper care ensures your SUKBIT bag maintains its beauty and durability for years to come. Natural fibers may develop a beautiful patina over time, adding character and telling the story of your journey together.

Our materials are naturally resistant to pests and mold due to their inherent properties, but they should be stored in dry, well-ventilated areas when not in use to maintain optimal condition.

Why Natural Materials Matter

In a world dominated by synthetic materials and fast fashion, choosing natural fibers is a statement of values. It's about supporting traditional crafts, protecting the environment, and investing in quality products that tell a story.

Every SUKBIT bag is a celebration of nature's bounty and human skill. When you carry our bags, you're not just carrying a fashion accessory – you're carrying a piece of nature, crafted by human hands, connecting you to the earth and to the rich traditions of Filipino craftsmanship.
