Choosing the right size ensures your SUKBIT bag perfectly suits your lifestyle and needs. Our handwoven bags come in various sizes, each designed for specific purposes while maintaining the beautiful craftsmanship and functionality you expect from SUKBIT.

How to Measure

To ensure the perfect fit, measure your current favorite bag and compare it to our size chart. Here's how to measure:

  • Length: Measure across the bottom of the bag from left to right
  • Width: Measure the depth of the bag from front to back
  • Height: Measure from the bottom to the top edge
  • Strap Drop: Measure from the top of the strap to the top of the bag

Size Selection Tips

  • For Daily Use: Choose Medium or Large sizes to accommodate phones, wallets, and daily essentials
  • For Travel: Consider Large or X-Large for extra space and versatility
  • For Evening: Mini or Small sizes are perfect for just the essentials
  • For Work: Medium to Large sizes can accommodate tablets and documents
  • Consider Your Lifestyle: Think about what you typically carry and choose accordingly
  • Room to Grow: Consider sizing up if you're between sizes or plan to carry more items

Custom Sizing

Because each SUKBIT bag is handwoven, we offer custom sizing options to perfectly match your needs. Whether you need a specific dimension for a particular purpose or prefer a different proportion, our artisans can create a bag tailored to your specifications.

Custom sizing typically adds 2-3 weeks to production time and may involve additional costs. Contact us to discuss your custom size requirements.

Size Adjustments Over Time

Natural fiber bags may experience minimal stretching over time with regular use. This is a natural characteristic of handwoven materials and adds to the unique character of your bag. The stretching is usually minimal (1-2 cm) and doesn't affect the bag's functionality or appearance.

If you have concerns about sizing or need assistance choosing the perfect size, don't hesitate to contact our customer service team. We're here to help you find the ideal SUKBIT bag for your lifestyle!
