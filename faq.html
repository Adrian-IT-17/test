Products & Materials

Our bags are handwoven from sustainable, locally-sourced materials including abaca fibers, native palm leaves, and other natural fibers. All materials are eco-friendly and biodegradable.

While our natural fiber bags are not completely waterproof, they are water-resistant to some degree. We recommend avoiding prolonged exposure to heavy rain and storing your bag in a dry place when not in use.

Gently spot clean with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid harsh chemicals or submerging the bag in water. Allow to air dry completely away from direct sunlight.
