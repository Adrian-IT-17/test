Care Guide

Caring for Your SUKBIT Bag

Your SUKBIT bag is crafted from natural fibers with care and precision. With proper care, your handwoven bag will maintain its beauty and durability for years to come. Follow these guidelines to keep your bag looking its best.

Daily Care

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Allow air to circulate around the bag when not in use
  • Stuff with tissue paper to maintain shape when stored
  • Avoid overloading to prevent stretching
  • Keep away from sharp objects that could snag fibers

Cleaning

  • Spot clean with a soft, damp cloth and mild soap
  • Test cleaning solutions on inconspicuous areas first
  • Blot gently - never rub vigorously
  • Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach
  • Never submerge your bag in water

Drying

  • Air dry naturally in a well-ventilated area
  • Keep away from direct heat sources or sunlight
  • Reshape while damp to maintain original form
  • Allow to dry completely before storage
  • Drying time varies from 12-24 hours depending on humidity

Protection

  • Use protective spray for water resistance (optional)
  • Avoid contact with makeup, perfumes, or oils
  • Keep away from rough surfaces that could cause abrasion
  • Use dust bags for long-term storage
  • Rotate between bags to extend their lifespan

Do's and Don'ts

✓ Do

  • Handle with clean, dry hands
  • Brush gently with a soft brush to remove dust
  • Store in breathable fabric bags
  • Address stains immediately
  • Enjoy the natural aging process

✗ Don't

  • Expose to prolonged direct sunlight
  • Store in plastic bags
  • Use harsh cleaning chemicals
  • Overstuff or overload
  • Ignore small damages - repair promptly

Professional Care Tips

For tough stains: Mix a small amount of mild soap with water, test on an inconspicuous area, then gently dab the stain with a soft cloth. Rinse with a clean damp cloth and air dry.

For odors: Place your bag in a well-ventilated area with baking soda or activated charcoal for 24 hours. Avoid using chemical fresheners.

For shape maintenance: Stuff your bag with acid-free tissue paper when storing long-term, and reshape after each use to maintain its form.

For minor repairs: Small snags can often be gently tucked back into place with a fine needle. For major repairs, contact us for professional restoration services.

Understanding Natural Aging

Like all natural materials, your SUKBIT bag will develop a unique patina over time. This natural aging process adds character and tells the story of your journey together. The fibers may soften slightly, and colors may deepen subtly - these are signs of a well-loved, well-used bag.

Embrace this evolution as part of your bag's unique story. With proper care, your SUKBIT bag will not only maintain its functionality but will grow more beautiful with each passing year, becoming a cherished companion that carries not just your belongings, but memories of your adventures together.

When to Seek Professional Help

While most maintenance can be done at home, certain situations require professional attention. Contact us if your bag shows signs of:

  • Significant structural damage or broken weaving
  • Persistent stains that won't respond to gentle cleaning
  • Mold or mildew growth
  • Severe water damage
  • Loose or broken straps and hardware

We offer professional restoration services to help maintain your SUKBIT bag in perfect condition for years to come.
