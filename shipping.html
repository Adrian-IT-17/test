Shipping Information

How We Get Your SUKBIT to You

We ship our handwoven bags worldwide with care and attention to ensure they arrive safely at your doorstep. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process.

Domestic Shipping (Philippines)

Delivery Time: 3-7 business days

Cost: ₱150 for Metro Manila, ₱200 for provincial areas

Courier: LBC, JRS Express, or 2GO

Tracking: Available for all orders

International Shipping

Delivery Time: 2-4 weeks

Cost: Varies by destination (calculated at checkout)

Courier: Philippines Post or international carriers

Tracking: Available for most destinations

Shipping Rates

Important Notes

• International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

• Rural and remote areas may experience longer delivery times.

• We carefully package each bag to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

• Express shipping options are available for domestic orders - please contact us for rates.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships. Custom orders may require additional processing time.

Package Tracking

All orders include tracking information. You'll receive an email with your tracking number once your package ships. You can track your order on our website or directly with the courier.

Lost or Damaged Packages

While we take every precaution to ensure safe delivery, occasionally packages may be lost or damaged in transit. Please contact us within 7 days of expected delivery if you haven't received your package or if it arrives damaged.
